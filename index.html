Membership and Nature of Business

The general purpose of this corporation is to carry the message of Alcoholics Anonymous to those with the disease of Alcoholism; to disseminate all the information thereto to the members; to provide and maintain facilities for necessary group therapy required in the progress of rehabilitation; but no facilities shall be provided for medical treatment or lodging.
